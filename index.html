Welcome to my home page!

Currently, I'm an M.Sc. student of Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). My research interests are in the areas of

  • Statistical Signal Processing
  • Image Processing
  • Machine Learning
  • Computer Vision

More importantly, I am interested in applying statistical methods and techniques to solve problems related to pattern classification. In addition, I am very motivated to implement unsupervised learning—mainly deep learning—algorithms in different pattern recognition problems and keen on theoretical aspects of deep learning.

Previously, I completed my undergraduate in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) from BUET, where I worked with Prof. S. M. Mahbubur Rahman (google scolar). Before that, I completed H.S.C. from Notre Dame College. Please have a look at my research page for my current and previous projects and publications.
